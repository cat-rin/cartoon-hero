Sailor Moon

About Sailor Moon

Sailor Moon is the secret identity of Usagi Tsukino. Sailor Moon is the leader of the Sailor Warriors. Her attacks are based on the moonlight, energy in different forms, healing, and sound. Usagi first started out as a whiny and sometimes a bit selfish girl. She was obsessed with boys and love, always desiring to to find a boyfriend for herself. Eventually she matured into a confident, strong woman, who embraced her powers as a sailor senshi. And she was willing to fight for her friends and the safety of the universe.
